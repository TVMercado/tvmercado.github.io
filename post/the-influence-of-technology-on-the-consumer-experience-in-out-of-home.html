The Influence of Technology on the Consumer Experience in Out-of-Home

Technology has significantly changed the way people consume in public spaces. With the expansion of mobile device use and increased availability of Wi-Fi networks, consumers now expect a more personalized, interactive, and rewarding experience across all communication channels.

Consumer Behavior Impact

However, technology not only changed how people consume, but also how they behave. Studies show that consumers become more loyal to brands that offer personalized and relevant experiences. Additionally, the ability to make online purchases and mobile payments allows people to shop 24/7 without having to commit to a single communication channel.

Evolution of Out-of-Home Advertising

Out-of-Home (OOH) advertising has also been affected by technology. OOH now includes all forms of non-print and digital advertising, such as billboards, ticket kiosk displays, cinema advertising, and most of what consumers see on a daily basis. OOH allowed brands to reach their customers in public spaces, something that may not have been possible years ago.

Has technology changed how people consume in public spaces?

Yes, technology has completely changed the way people consume in public spaces. With the expansion of mobile device use and increased Wi-Fi availability, consumers now expect a more personalized, interactive, and rewarding experience across all communication channels.

As marketing, it's crucial to understand how technology is changing the way people consume in public spaces, so that brands can adapt and offer more personalized and relevant experiences. With the evolution of mobile technologies and data, brands now have more tools to better understand their consumers and provide a customized experience.
