Outdoor Media: Understanding the Power of External Advertising

Out-of-home (OOH) advertising, also known as exterior advertising, is a type of advertising that does not go through television and is not digital. It involves placing ads in public locations, such as football stadiums, train stations, airports, parks, etc.

Out-of-home media has a significant impact, since the vast majority of people spend at least several hours a day in public places where they are exposed to advertisements. Moreover, outdoor advertising is not turned off, meaning that people experience it at specific moments.
