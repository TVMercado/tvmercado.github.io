Development of Governance Policies for AI

Importance of governance policies for AI

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is revolutionizing the way organizations operate, from decision-making to process automation. However, AI also presents risks, such as data loss, data breaches, and misuse. Therefore, it's essential to implement governance policies for AI to ensure transparency and accountability.

Research shows that organizations that implement governance policies for AI have better performance and reduce the risks associated with automation.

What are AI governance policies?

AI governance policies are guidelines that regulate the use of AI within the organization, ensuring data security and confidentiality. This includes authorization to use AI, defining responsibilities, and regulating privileges.
