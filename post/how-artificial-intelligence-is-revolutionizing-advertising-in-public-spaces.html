How artificial intelligence is revolutionizing advertising in public spaces

Artificial intelligence (AI) has been one of the biggest technological revolutions in recent years, and its application in outdoor advertising is no exception. According to a study conducted by Publicitas, 75% of entrepreneurs consider AI to be essential for the success of outdoor advertising.

Data analysis for creating experiences

One of the greatest advantages of artificial intelligence in public space advertising is its ability to analyze data. With AI, it's possible to collect and analyze large volumes of data about customers, including information such as location, time, and preferences. These informations can be used to create more personalized and effective advertising experiences.

But is that all artificial intelligence can do for advertising in public spaces?

No, it can also be used to monitor and manage ad campaigns, identify opportunities for increasing effectiveness and optimize the distribution of ads. Moreover, artificial intelligence can help predict how consumers will respond to different types of advertising, which may allow companies to adjust their strategies in real-time.

In addition, artificial intelligence can be used to encrypt ads according to consumer rotation, which may help capture the attention of consumers and increase the effectiveness of advertising.

The benefits of artificial intelligence in advertising in public spaces

Beyond improving the effectiveness of public advertising, artificial intelligence can bring other benefits, such as cost reduction, consumer experience improvement, and the possibility of obtaining faster and more precise results.

Are there risks associated with using artificial intelligence in public advertising spaces?

Yes, it's important to consider risks such as loss of control over data collection, privacy violations, and the possibility of detection errors in autonomous systems. However, by working with specialized companies and AI solution providers, these risks can be minimized.

In summary, artificial intelligence is revolutionizing advertising in public spaces, improving the effectiveness and experience of consumers. Although there are risks associated, the use of artificial intelligence can lead to faster, more precise, and more effective results.
