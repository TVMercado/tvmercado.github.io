Data analysis to optimize the effectiveness of outdoor advertising campaigns in DOOH

Outdoor advertising campaigns on digital billboards (DOOH) have become a very useful tool for brands and companies that want to reach a specific audience and increase their visibility in public spaces. However, for these campaigns to be effective, it is crucial to analyze the data and optimize the marketing strategy.

Why analyzing data is crucial

Data analysis is crucial to understand the performance of your outdoor advertising campaigns in DOOH. This is because, without data, it's impossible to know if the marketing strategy is reaching its goals. With data analysis, you can identify which advertisements are most effective, which locations are most visited, and what times are best for advertising.

What are the main indicators to be analyzed?

There are several metrics that need to be analyzed to optimize the effectiveness of DOOH advertising campaigns. Some of the most important indicators include: visibility rate, interaction rate, exposure time, and click-through number. Additionally, it is essential to analyze demographic and psychographic audience data to better understand the needs of the target audience.

What is the best time for advertising on outdoor screen panels?

Timing advertising in DOOH is crucial to reach the target audience. In general, the most effective hours for advertising in DOOH are those that coincide with peak traffic periods at public locations, such as afternoons and weekends.

Another important tip is not to forget to adjust the marketing strategy in response to the data. This can be done by changing the location of the advertisement, the broadcast time or the actual advertisement message. With the help of data analysis, it's possible to adjust the marketing strategy and increase the effectiveness of DOOH advertising campaigns.

What are the main tools for analyzing advertising campaign data in DOOH (Digital Out of Home)?

There are several tools that can be used for analyzing advertising campaign data in DOOH. Some of the main ones including: Google Analytics, Facebook Audience Insights, DataHive, DataSift, and Salesforce Einstein...
