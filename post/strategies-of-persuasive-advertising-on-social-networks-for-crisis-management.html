Strategies of Persuasive Advertising on Social Networks for Crisis Management

In today's scenario, social media is a fundamental means of communication and advertising. However, when a crisis occurs, how a brand or company handles it can be decisive for its reputation and legitimacy. In this context, persuasive advertising on social media has become an essential strategy for crisis management.

What is persuasive advertising?

Persuasive advertising is a marketing strategy that relies on persuasive techniques to influence public opinion and consumer opinions about a brand or company.

In the event of a crisis, persuasive advertising can be an essential tool for handling the situation, as it allows the brand or company to add value to its storytelling and change how the crisis is perceived by public opinion.

How to use persuasive advertising to manage crises?

Here are some strategies for using persuasive advertising on social media to manage crises:

Confirm the authority and credibility - The authority and credibility of a brand or company are essential for handling crises. Use persuasive advertising to reinforce the authority and credibility of the brand or company.

Focus on solving the problem - Rather than just dealing with the effects of the crisis, persuasive advertising can help a brand or company focus on solving the problem and the future.

Storytelling is crucial for persuasion. Use persuasive advertising to create a narrative that explains what happened and how the brand or company is handling the crisis.

Be transparent - Transparency is crucial for handling crises. Use persuasive advertising to explain what happened and how the brand or company is dealing with the situation.

Use the data to demonstrate the positive impact - The use of data and numbers can help improve the brand or company's reputation and demonstrate the positive impact of crisis management efforts.

Focus on problem-solving - Instead of just dealing with the effects of the crisis, persuasive advertising can help the brand or company focus on solving the problem and looking to the future.

Seek cooperation - Instead of trying to deal with the crisis alone, seek cooperation and support to handle the situation.

Stay ready for changes - The crisis is a dynamic process and persuasive advertising can be adjusted as the situation unfolds.

Work with a team - Persuasive advertising can be more effective when working with a team that has different skills and unique perspectives.

Learn from mistakes - Learn from past crises and adjust the strategy according to what was learned.
