Proximity Advertising Trends and Out-of-Home

Proximity advertising and out-of-home (outdoor) are two marketing strategies that have been gaining more attention in recent years. With the evolution of technology and changes in consumer behavior, these forms of marketing start to gain a new life. Here are some trends that are dominating proximity advertising and out-of-home.

The use of IoT in proximity advertising

The Internet of Things (IoT) is revolutionizing the way market publicists connect with consumers. Proximity advertising can now include the use of technologies like Wi-Fi and Bluetooth to collect data on consumers and personalize the advertising experience.

What is Out-of-Home?

Out-of-Home (OOH) is a form of advertising that refers to ads and promotional products in public spaces, such as streets, parks, train stations, airports, and other high-traffic areas for people. These ads can include slogans, images, text messages and/or vibrant colors to attract the attention of consumers.

The use of data-driven marketing in proximity advertising and out-of-home

The ability to store and analyze data on consumers is crucial for the effectiveness of proximity advertising and out-of-home marketing. The data-driven marketing technique allows brands to gather data and tools to better understand consumers and create more attractive and personalized ads.

The increase in women's participation in sports and leisure activities

Women are becoming increasingly participatory in sports and leisure areas, making them an important target for proximity advertising and out-of-home. For example, walking and cycling are becoming more popular, which creates opportunities for proximity advertising in parks and trails.

Why proximity advertising and out-of-home advertising are becoming more popular?

Proximity advertising and out-of-home advertising are becoming more popular due to their high exposure value and the ability to reach consumers in just one location. Additionally, proximity advertising and out-of-home advertising allow brands to create memorable advertising experiences and build stronger relationships with consumers.

Conclusion

Proximity advertising and out-of-home advertising are evolving to adapt to the needs of companies and consumers. With the use of more advanced technologies and data analysis, brands can improve the effectiveness of their advertising strategy and build a closer relationship with customers. In summary, proximity advertising and out-of-home advertising are effective marketing strategies that should be considered by companies.
