Sustainable Development for Youth

Introduction

Sustainable development is a concept that gained great notoriety in recent years, especially since the adoption of the United Nations' Agenda 2030. However, what many people don't know is that youth have a fundamental role in this process. As the next generation that will surround and inherit the Earth, young people need to be connected and involved in decisions that affect the future.

What is Sustainable Development?

Sustainable development is a model of development that combines economic growth with the preservation of the environment and the reduction of social inequalities. It's a broad concept that addresses the interaction between humans and the environment, seeking to balance current needs with those of the future.

Why is sustainable development important for youth?

The importance of sustainable development for youth can be understood in various aspects. First and foremost, young people are the main workforce within a few years. Additionally, youth is responsible for 15% of global energy consumption, while accounting for 30% of the world's population, and these numbers are growing. Finally, youth has great influence on standards and decisions that affect the future.

The importance of young people for sustainability

Young people have a different view of the world and its sustainability. They are more inclined to worry about the environment and seek solutions for environmental problems. Moreover, youth has a powerful role in promoting change, since they are more connected and more willing to make changes.

How can youth contribute to sustainability?

Youth can contribute to sustainability in several ways. First, they can get involved in environmental initiatives, such as beach or forest cleaning projects, or participate in social movements that promote change. Additionally, they can become entrepreneurs and start businesses that promote sustainability. Finally, they can serve as environmental leaders and promote responsible use of resources.

Can young people make a difference?

Yes, young people can make a difference. They are the most technological and connected generation; they can use their skills and knowledge to promote sustainability and change the world. Moreover, active youth participation in political processes and decision-making can influence the creation of more sustainable policies.

Conclusion

Youth plays a fundamental role in sustainable development and their participation is essential for its success. It's important that young people are involved in decisions that affect the future and have a voice in building the world we will inherit.

Therefore, it is essential to promote awareness and employability of young people in these areas, as well as provide opportunities for them to engage and develop themselves in that field.
